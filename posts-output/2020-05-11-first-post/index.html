First post

I've been kicking around the idea of starting a blog for some time now. I'm hoping to share some random ramblings here, partly to share my observations and partly just to record them.

Topics

I certainly plan to write about programming. But I'd like to talk about often overlooked issues for programmers. Things I've found noteworthy enough to share with you.

  • languages
  • tools
  • productivity
  • tips
  • observations

Ideas for next post

We all likely have the 'essential' tools required for our job:

  • email
  • feature/ticket system
  • VCS
  • chat

But there are a lot of gaps. What about your personal process? How do you capture additional knowledge for future work on your project? How do you track status in a more granular way, particularly if it is not imposed by your organization? Do you even need any more structure?
